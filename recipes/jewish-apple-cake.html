Jewish Apple Cake

Home

Description:

This delicious cake is made with orange juice and oil instead of milk and butter. If you have trouble with the cake falling, try baking it in a bundt pan -- the end result is turned upside-down so it will look perfect!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large apples, peeled and sliced thin
  • 2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 5 tbsp. sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup oil
  • 4 eggs, unbeaten
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice or the juice of one orange
  • 2 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 3 tsp. baking powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Soak apples in cinnamon and 5 tbsp sugar while preparing the batter.
  3. Mix all ingredients (apart from apples mixture) together in a bowl. Beat until smooth.
  4. Grease tube pan or bundt pan.
  5. Beginning with batter, layer batter, apples, batter, apples.
  6. Bake for 1.75 to 2 hours.
  7. Allow to cool slightly before serving.
