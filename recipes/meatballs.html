Meatballs

Home

Description:

These Italian meatballs include a delicious ingredient: Romano cheese. Feel free to add addtional seasonings to taste!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 cups Italian bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Romano cheese
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. pepper
  • 1/8 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Shape into 1" balls. Tip: use a melon baller or a cookie scoop. You should get about 25 meatballs.
  3. Heat oil in a pan on Med to Med-High heat until hot (check temperature by adding a few drops of water. It will sizzle when hot).
  4. Place meatballs in one layer in pan. Do not pack in tightly. You may need to cook the meatballs in batches.
  5. Cook the meatballs until brown on all sides, rotating to ensure even cooking. The internal temperature should reach 165 degrees F.
  6. Add to spaghetti sauce, use in meatball subs, or freeze for later.
