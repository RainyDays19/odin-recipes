Brownies

Home

Description:

These amazing brownies are a cross between traditional dense brownies and chocolate cake. Not too dense, not too airy, they are the perfect texture and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup butter (1 stick)
  • 2 oz. unsweetened chocolate (2 squares)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts, if desired (walnuts or pecans)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Grease and lightly flour bottom only of 8- or 9-inch square pan
  3. In large saucepan, melt butter and chocolate over low heat, stirring constantly.
  4. Remove from heat; cool slightly.
  5. Blend in sugar.
  6. Beat in eggs, one at a time.
  7. Stir in remaining ingredients.
  8. Spread into prepared pan.
  9. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until set in center.
  10. Cool; cut into bars.
